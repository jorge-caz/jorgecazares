Vision

ScholarSeed aims to revolutionize math education by transforming the way people learn mathematics. Like Duolingo did for languages, we're creating an engaging, bite-sized learning experience that helps users overcome math anxiety and build genuine confidence with numbers through progressive, gamified lessons.

Our freemium model makes quality math education accessible to everyone, with premium features for deeper practice and advanced topics. We believe everyone can become good at math with the right approach and consistent practice.

Development Approach

ScholarSeed is currently in the conceptual and planning phase. The vision is to create a comprehensive math learning platform that addresses the widespread issue of math anxiety through proven gamification techniques and adaptive learning.

The planned architecture centers around the MERN stack for rapid development and scalability, with Stripe integration for seamless premium subscription management. Key technical challenges will include creating an adaptive algorithm that personalizes difficulty progression, implementing engaging gamification mechanics, and building a robust content management system for mathematical concepts and exercises.

The freemium model will ensure accessibility while providing sustainable revenue through premium features like advanced practice modes, detailed progress analytics, and specialized content for competitive math and standardized tests.
